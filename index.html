Painting your home--how not to mess it up

Painting sucks. It sucks even more when you're trying to paint a sharpe line and paint bleeds under the tape.

Getting through it-how to bribe your friends with food and drink to help paint

Whilst painting your home, help is, well, helpful.
So, here are some tips on getting your friends to help with the awful task of painting before they really know what they're getting into:

  • Bribe with booze and pizza
  • Talk about how fun it will be
  • Lie about the amount of time you need their help and be sure to remove clocks and phones to remove evidence of time.

What happens when you've messed up and your wall looks a 3rd grader tried to painted an elephant with his face

  1. Don't. Panic.
  2. Go buy some primer. A lot of primer.
  3. Take a deep breath and remember it's just a wall and the primer will fix it. Eventually.
